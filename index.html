Features
XP
XP & Streaks
Earn XP from commits, file edits, and session time. Daily streaks multiply your gains up to 2x.
EV
Evolution
4 stages gated by XP, active days, and streak. Consistency beats grinding.
MO
Mood System
Happy pet = bonus XP. Sad pet = penalty. Your activity shapes their mood.
EV
Random Events
Coding Storm (2x XP), Code Drought, Good Vibes. A new event rolls each day.
AC
Accessories
10 pixel art items and 4 backgrounds. Unlock with XP, equip from the menu.
DC
Decay & Revival
Miss days and your pet gets sick. 7 days absent = runs away. Code 30 min to revive.
